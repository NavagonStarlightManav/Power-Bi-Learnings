# Data Jobs Dashboard w/ Power Bi 
## Introduction 

![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Data%20Jobs%20Dashboard.png)

Providing In-Depth analysis of about the data job market making it easier to grasp and analyize 
It Provides a single , easy-to-use interface to explore market trends and compensation .

## Skills showcased 
**Core Charts** : Utilized **Column , Bar , line charts** and **Area charts** to compare job counts and track trends over time 

**Geo-spatial Analysis** : Leveraged **Map Charts** to visualize the global distribution of Jobs . 

**Tables** to showcase granular , sortable data and **Cards** were used to display key metrics 

**Dashboard Design** : Designed an intuitive and visually appealing layout , exploring both common and uncommon chart types to best tell the data story 

**Additional Features** : Slicers , buttons , bookmarks and Drill through to provide a seamless experience

### Conclusion

This dashboard showcases how Power BI can transform raw job posting data into a powerful tool for career analysis . it allows users to slice , filter , and drill through data to make informed decisions about their career paths .
